TAROT OF COCKTAILS

A delicious drink is in the cards.

Tarot of Cocktails is an original drink recipe deck. Each of the 30 cards features a tarot-inspired illustration on one side and a craft cocktail recipe on the other. The deck includes a companion booklet featuring home bartending basics, tips & tricks for each recipe, a place to take notes and more.

The Cards

Each card began as a hand-drawn illustration. Starting with the basic composition of a classic tarot card, the creator would dream up new imagery based on the cocktail recipe. Although inspired by the beauty of tarot, the deck is designed for delicious drinks, not divination.

  • The Drinks

    Created with the casual connoisseur in mind, the deck’s drink recipes are a mix of imaginative originals and riffs on classics. The aspirational yet accessible selection includes stiff sippers, boozy floats, light summery drinks and more.

The Story

A multi-faceted creative based in Portland, OR, Katy combined her knack for craft cocktails with her admiration of classic tarot cards to bring this dream project to life. As soon as she dove into the project, it seemed that Tarot of Cocktails was written in the stars. From drawing the cards to formulating the recipes, it was a labor of love and she hopes you love it!
