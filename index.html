Tarot of Cocktails

Tarot of Cocktails is an original drink recipe deck. Each of the 30 cards features a tarot-inspired illustration on one side and a craft cocktail recipe on the other. The deck includes a companion booklet featuring home bartending basics, tips & tricks for each recipe, a place to take notes and more.

The Cards

Each card began as a hand-drawn illustration. Starting with the basic composition of a classic tarot card, the creator would dream up new imagery based on the cocktail recipe. Although inspired by the beauty of tarot, the deck is designed for delicious drinks, not divination.
